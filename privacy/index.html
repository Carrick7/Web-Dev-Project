Privacy Policy

This is our Privacy Policy. It explains what we do with your personal data. It details how much of your personal data we collect about you and what we do with that data. It also explains your rights under the law including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Information We Collect

When you fill in our Sign Up form, we collect your name, email address and date of birth. When you engage with our services, we also collect other information about you. When you complete one of our quizzes, we store your results, including which answers you got right and wrong.

As you engage with our website, we also collect the following further information about you:

    Usage Patterns
  • Time spend on each question
  • Clicks
  • Mouse Movements
    Technical Specifications
  • Your IP Address
  • Your Device Type
  • Your Operating System

Information We Share With Third Parties

With your consent, we may share your quiz results with third parties. For example, you may wish to share your results with a prospective employer or with a tutor. If you choose to do so, we will provide those third parties with a certified copy of your results. In order to consent to this sharing of your results, please enable the toggle below.

Tick to enable sharing your results:

You have enabled sharing your results. You can diable this feature at any time by unticking the box.

To allow a third party to access your results, you must generate a unique code. Share this code with the person you wish to share your results with. They must quote this code, along with your username, when contacting us.

Your Rights

You have a number of rights regarding the processing of your personal data as provided by the GDPR. These rights include:

  1. The right to be informed
  2. The right of access
  3. The right to rectification
  4. The right to erasure
  5. The right to restrict processing
  6. The right to data portability
  7. The right to object
  8. Rights in relation to automated decision making and profiling
